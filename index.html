Brownies

This page is about brownies

Brownies are a beloved dessert known for their rich, chocolatey flavor and dense, fudgy texture. They are typically made with a blend of cocoa powder or melted chocolate, butter, sugar, eggs, and flour.

Originating in the United States, brownies have become a worldwide favorite, with countless variations including nut-studded, caramel-swirled, or even cream cheese-topped versions. Perfect as a snack or dessert, brownies can be enjoyed plain, dusted with powdered sugar, or served warm with a scoop of ice cream.
